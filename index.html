📄 Plan of Action (POA) — Inauthentic Complaint
For ASIN deactivations due to authenticity concerns
›
Dear Amazon Selling Partner Support, We are writing to appeal the deactivation of ASIN [ASIN] — [Product Name] under our seller account [Seller Account Name / ID]. ROOT CAUSE: [Describe the root cause — e.g., "We received an inauthentic complaint from a buyer regarding ASIN [ASIN]. After investigation, we identified that the complaint stemmed from [reason — e.g., a packaging variation between lot numbers that caused confusion]."] IMMEDIATE CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN: 1. We immediately removed the affected listing from active sale pending investigation. 2. We reviewed all current inventory at [FBA warehouse / our warehouse] and verified product authenticity. 3. We contacted our supplier [Supplier Name] to obtain the necessary documentation. PREVENTIVE MEASURES GOING FORWARD: 1. We will require updated purchase invoices and Certificates of Authenticity (COA) from all suppliers prior to any future purchase orders. 2. We will conduct product quality checks before each FBA shipment. 3. We will implement a batch tracking system to trace all inventory to its source. SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS ATTACHED: - Purchase Invoice from [Supplier Name] dated [Date] - Supplier Letter of Authorization - [Any additional COA or lab results if applicable] We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience caused and request reinstatement of the ASIN at the earliest. We are fully committed to maintaining Amazon's high standards and protecting our customers. Best regards, [Your Name] [Company Name] [Seller Account Name] [Email] | [Phone]
📄 Certificate of Authenticity (COA) Appeal
When COA documentation is submitted as evidence
›
Dear Amazon Seller Performance Team, We are submitting this appeal with supporting documentation for the deactivation of ASIN [ASIN] — [Product Name]. We have attached the following documents to verify the authenticity of our products: 1. Purchase Invoice from [Authorized Distributor/Manufacturer Name] — dated [Date] 2. Certificate of Authenticity / Letter of Authorization from the brand owner 3. [Lab test results / GMP certification — if applicable] The products were sourced directly from [Supplier/Manufacturer Name], an authorized distributor of [Brand Name]. All products sold under our account are genuine, unopened, and in original manufacturer packaging. We request a full review of the attached documentation and reinstatement of ASIN [ASIN]. We are happy to provide additional documentation if required. Thank you for your time and consideration. Sincerely, [Your Name] [Company Name / Seller Account] [Contact Email]
📄 IP Infringement / Rights Owner Complaint Appeal
For trademark, copyright, or patent violation notices
›
Dear Amazon Seller Performance Team, We are writing to appeal the removal of ASIN [ASIN] — [Product Name] due to an intellectual property complaint filed by [Rights Owner Name / Brand]. EXPLANATION: [Option A — Retraction: We have contacted [Rights Owner Name] directly and they have agreed to retract the complaint. A retraction notice will be submitted / has been submitted by the rights owner directly to Amazon.] [Option B — Authorization: We are an authorized reseller of [Brand Name] products. Attached is our Letter of Authorization from [Brand Name / Manufacturer], confirming our right to sell their products on Amazon.] [Option C — No Infringement: After careful review, we believe our listing does not infringe on any intellectual property. Our product is [description — e.g., "a generic/private label product that does not use any trademarks, patents, or copyrighted material belonging to the complainant"]."] CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN: 1. We have reviewed our entire catalog for any potential IP conflicts. 2. We have updated our listing to ensure it does not include any infringing content. 3. We have established a process to verify IP rights before listing any product. ATTACHED: - [Letter of Authorization OR Retraction Letter from Rights Owner OR evidence of no infringement] We respectfully request reinstatement of this ASIN and are happy to provide further documentation. Best regards, [Your Name] [Company Name] [Seller Account]
📄 Product Safety Complaint Appeal
For deactivations due to safety concerns or recalls
›
Dear Amazon Seller Performance Team, We are writing to appeal the deactivation of ASIN [ASIN] — [Product Name] due to a product safety concern. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS: [Describe the reported safety issue and your investigation — e.g., "We received a complaint regarding [safety issue]. Upon thorough investigation, we determined that [the issue was isolated to a single batch / the product meets all safety standards / the complaint was based on misuse]."] CORRECTIVE ACTIONS TAKEN: 1. Immediately removed the listing from active sale pending investigation. 2. Reviewed all FBA inventory and [quarantined affected batch / confirmed product safety]. 3. Contacted our manufacturer [Manufacturer Name] for updated safety certifications. 4. Obtained updated [safety testing results / compliance documentation]. SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS: - Product Safety Test Report from [Accredited Lab Name] — dated [Date] - Compliance Certification: [e.g., FDA registration, CPSC compliance, SDS sheet] - Manufacturer's Quality Assurance Documentation PREVENTIVE MEASURES: 1. All new inventory will undergo pre-shipment safety inspections. 2. We will maintain a product recall monitoring process. 3. We will immediately notify Amazon of any product safety issues discovered. We are committed to customer safety and Amazon's product quality standards. We respectfully request reinstatement of this ASIN after review. Best regards, [Your Name] [Company Name / Seller Account]
